About

Sage Charie Monroe is the literary voice of Dr. Charie Poderoso, Ed.D.—an educator, speaker, and guide for women reclaiming their worth. Sage writes at the intersection of heartbreak and healing, identity and reinvention. Her debut work, *Anatomy of No Contact*, is a soulful blend of lived experience and grounded wisdom, offering readers a path to rediscover their inner power.

Books

  • Anatomy of No Contact – A hybrid memoir and guide for women navigating silence, heartbreak, and self-reclamation.
  • The Silence That Saved Her (Coming Soon) – A psychological fiction debut that explores trauma, lineage, and feminine power.
